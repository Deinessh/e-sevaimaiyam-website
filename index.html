Who We Are

E-Sevai Maiyam (Common Services Centre) Minjur

Common Services Centres (CSC) act as the physical access points for delivery of essential digital public utilities, social welfare schemes, healthcare, financial, education, and agriculture services to citizens.

Our mission is to establish a transparent, simplified service delivery mechanism that reduces a citizen's time and effort in visiting various government offices. We handle end-to-end documentation, filing, verification support, and final printouts under one roof.

Secure & Encrypted Portals

All data and attachments are securely processed through authenticated official state and central portal applications.

Certified Assistance

Our experienced officers are fully updated on government requirements, avoiding application rejections.
